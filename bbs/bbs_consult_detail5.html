Round table on the subject “The use of Big Data in education”（ Turan University）

432314Oct • 27 / 2017

http://www.turan-edu.kz/en/round-table-on-the-subject-the-use-of-big-data-in-education/

2017年10月27日，“计算机与软件工程”系举办“教育大数据使用”圆桌会议。目前的大数据是信息技术发展的关键驱动力之一。许多人认为“大数据”只是数据的累积量，而忘记了这个数据包括存储，计算和服务的技术。

在教育方面，我们可以确定几个领域，他们可以积累大量结构性和结构不良的电子数据：在线学习系统;学生和教师的科学活动信息;学生课外活动;雇主对学生就业的要求;有关学前教育和学校教育的数据。

原文：October 27, 2017, the Department “Computer and software engineering” organized a round table on “the Use of big data in education” with the participation of teachers and undergraduates of the Department “Computer and software engineering”, and also teachers of the Department “radio engineering, electronics and telecommunications” and “Information systems”.

Big Data at the moment are one of the key drivers of development of information technologies. Many believe that “Big Data” means only the amount of accumulated data, but forget that this area includes technologies of storage, computation and services.

In education, we can identify several areas where they can accumulate large amounts of structured and poorly structured electronic data: online learning system; information on scientific activities of students and teachers; extracurricular activities of students; the demands of employers about the employment of students; data on preschool and school education.
